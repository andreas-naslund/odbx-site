Privacy Policy for OBDX

Effective date: August 2025

OBDX is an iPhone app for consumer OBD2 diagnostics. This Privacy Policy explains how OBDX handles information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

OBDX does not require account creation and does not directly collect personal information through the app.

Vehicle details, scan history, diagnostic results, and related content created in the app are stored locally on your device to provide the app’s functionality.

How Information Is Used

Information stored in OBDX is used only to:

  • connect to a compatible external OBD2 adapter
  • run vehicle diagnostic scans
  • generate reports and repair guidance
  • save scan history on your device
  • support optional user-initiated sharing, copying, or AI handoff actions

Local Device Storage

Vehicle information, scan results, and related diagnostic content may be stored locally on your iPhone. This information remains on your device unless you choose to share it.

Sharing

OBDX only shares information when you explicitly choose to do so, such as when you:

  • share an AI brief
  • copy diagnostic content
  • use iOS sharing features

Purchases

OBDX may offer in-app purchases through Apple’s App Store. Purchase processing is handled by Apple and is subject to Apple’s terms, privacy policies, and systems. OBDX does not store your payment card information.

Support Emails

If you contact support by email, your email address and the information you include in your message will be used only to respond to your request.

Third-Party Services

OBDX may rely on Apple services such as the App Store and StoreKit for app distribution and in-app purchases. Those services are governed by Apple’s own terms and privacy policies.

Data Retention

Because OBDX primarily stores data locally on your device, you control how long that data remains there. You may remove locally stored app data by deleting the app, subject to any device backup settings you control.

Children’s Privacy

OBDX is not directed to children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: support@naslund.tv
