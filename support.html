OBDX Support

OBDX is an iPhone app for consumer OBD2 diagnostics. If you need help, want to report a bug, or have a question about a scan result, contact support by email.

Contact

Email: support@naslund.tv

Before You Email

  • Include your iPhone model and iOS version.
  • Describe the issue as clearly as possible.
  • Mention your vehicle make, model, and year if relevant.
  • Note which OBD2 adapter you are using.

Important

OBDX requires a compatible external OBD2 adapter. Vehicle compatibility can vary depending on the adapter and the car.
